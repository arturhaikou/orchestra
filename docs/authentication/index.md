# Authentication

The Authentication module handles everything related to your Orchestra identity — creating your account, signing in, and keeping your profile up to date. It is the gateway to all other parts of the platform.

## Guides

* [Sign Up](./sign-up/guide.md) — Create a new Orchestra account and enter the application immediately
* [Log In](./log-in/guide.md) — Sign in to your existing Orchestra account and access the application
* [Update Your Profile](./profile-updating/guide.md) — Change your display name, email address, or password
